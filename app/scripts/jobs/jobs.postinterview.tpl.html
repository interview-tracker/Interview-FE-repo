Here are some tips for the post-interview process...
  • Send a Thank You note!
  • Check back within a week to check on status!
  • Find areas that need improvement!
  • Gather any Business Card that you may need!
  • Talk with current Employees for insight!
  • Continue the Interview Process with other companies!
  • Record key takeaways from Interview!
  • Ask recruiter for feedback on Interview!
  • Record Pros and Cons of accepting the Job if offered!
  • Find out when they will make a decision and contact you!
