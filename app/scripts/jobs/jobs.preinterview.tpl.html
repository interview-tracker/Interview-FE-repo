Here are some Helpful tips for the pre-interview process...
  • Make a great first impression!
  • Arrive at least 15 minutes early!
  • Dress appropriately!
  • Carry extra resumes along with you!
  • Practice answering any interview questions!
  • Make eye contact!
  • Answer any questions you might encounter clearly and concisely.
  • Thank the Interviewer(s) for their time!
  • Give a firm handshake!
  • Listen carefully to each question!
